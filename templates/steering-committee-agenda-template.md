# Steering Committee Agenda Template

Use this template to structure steering committee meetings that oversee major initiatives and governance decisions.

A clear agenda helps ensure that governance meetings focus on **alignment, risk resolution, and decision-making**, rather than operational reporting.

---

## Meeting Information

Meeting Date  
Meeting Chair  
Participants  
Initiatives Covered  

---

## Agenda

### 1. Portfolio Overview

Provide a brief summary of the current initiative portfolio.

Topics may include:

- initiative status highlights
- overall portfolio health
- strategic alignment updates

---

### 2. Program Updates

Program leaders provide concise updates on major initiatives.

Updates should focus on:

- milestone progress
- major risks or dependencies
- decisions required from governance

---

### 3. Risks and Escalations

Discuss issues that require governance attention.

Examples may include:

- cross-department conflicts
- resource constraints
- delivery risks affecting strategic outcomes

---

### 4. Decisions Required

Identify decisions requiring steering committee approval.

Examples may include:

- scope adjustments
- prioritization of initiatives
- resource allocation decisions

---

### 5. Action Items

Capture follow-up actions resulting from meeting discussions.

---

### 6. Summary and Next Steps

Confirm key decisions made and actions assigned.

Review the next governance meeting date and any preparation required.
