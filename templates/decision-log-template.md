# Decision Log Template

Use this template to document significant decisions made during governance reviews or leadership meetings.

Maintaining a decision log helps organizations track decisions over time and ensures that leadership agreements are clearly documented and communicated.

---

## How to Use This Template

### Decision Date
Record the date when the decision was made.

### Decision
Provide a concise summary of the decision.

### Decision Owner
Identify the individual or leadership group responsible for the decision.

### Context
Briefly describe the situation or issue that required the decision.

### Outcome
Document the final decision or direction agreed upon.

### Follow-up Actions
Identify any actions required to implement the decision.

---

## Why Decision Logs Matter

Documenting governance decisions helps organizations:

- maintain transparency across leadership groups  
- avoid revisiting previously resolved issues  
- track decisions affecting major initiatives  
- ensure accountability for follow-up actions  

Decision logs are often reviewed during governance meetings to confirm progress on previous decisions.
