# Governance Dashboard Template

Use this template to summarize the health and progress of major initiatives across an organizational portfolio.

A governance dashboard provides leadership with a concise overview of active initiatives, highlighting progress, risks, and decisions requiring attention.

The goal of the dashboard is to help leadership quickly understand **portfolio health and priorities** without reviewing detailed program reports.
